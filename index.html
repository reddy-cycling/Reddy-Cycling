🚴‍♂️ About Our Cycle Group 🚴‍♀️

The Reddy Cycle Group consists of seven members, each contributing to the group's success. Shriram Yelgate holds the position of leader, guiding and coordinating the group's activities. The other dedicated members include Manoj Kuchame, Venket Gomage, Narsinha Gomare, Dayanand Kodbole, Praven Gomare, and Mohan Gomare. Together, they work cohesively to achieve their goals and promote cycling as a healthy and eco-friendly mode of transportation in their community. With Shriram Yelgate's leadership, this group is undoubtedly making a positive impact on cycling enthusiasts in their area.

🏆 Our Achievements 🚴

Celebrating milestones that define our journey.

Tirupati Tour

Pedaling 950 kilometers from Latur to Tirupati with a seven-member group is an invigorating adventure, forging lasting bonds of camaraderie and culminating in the spiritual embrace of the Venkateswara Temple atop the Tirumala Hills.

Team Members: All Members

100 Consecutive Days

Embarking on a 100 consecutive days challenge, whether it be for fitness, learning, or personal growth, represents a remarkable journey of commitment, discipline, and continuous effort.

Team Members: All Members

200 km in a Single Ride

Achieving a 200-kilometer distance in a single ride is a testament to both physical endurance and mental resilience, marking a significant milestone in the realm of long-distance cycling.

Team Members: All Members

Latur to Tuljapur

Pedaling from Latur to Tuljapur, a 100-kilometer journey, unveils the picturesque rural landscapes and culminates at the sacred Tulja Bhavani Temple.

Team Members: All Memebers

Latur to Pandharpur

Covering the distance from Latur to Pandharpur, a journey of around 250 kilometers, offers a pilgrimage-like experience as cyclists traverse through diverse landscapes, connecting urban Latur to the spiritual town of Pandharpur, known for the Vithoba temple.

Team Members: All Team Memebers and Latur Cycling Group
